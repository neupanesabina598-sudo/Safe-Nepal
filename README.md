

**Safe Nepal** is a browser-based geospatial web application developed to improve tourist safety and civic reporting across Nepal. Built using Leaflet.js for interactive mapping and Turf.js for spatial analysis, the platform allows tourists and local citizens to explore destinations, submit location-based safety and civic reports, and perform spatial operations such as distance measurement, buffer analysis, and nearest location finding. It covers all administrative levels from country down to ward, and includes features like live search, route planning, a reporting dashboard, and CSV export. The project was developed by Sabina Neupane and Dipesh Prajapati, students of Geomatics Engineering at Kathmandu University, under the supervision of Er. Suman Baral, with the motto *"Mapping Accountability Across Nepal."*
