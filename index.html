📖 About Safe Nepal

Safe Nepal is an interactive web-based GIS platform designed to bridge the gap between public grievances and government authorities through location-based reporting. The platform empowers both tourists and local residents to report safety concerns, infrastructure issues, and travel-related problems by pinning exact coordinates on an interactive map.

🎯 Purpose

The purpose of Safe Nepal is to transform traditional text-based complaint systems into a visual, location-specific, and action-oriented platform. By integrating geospatial technology, the platform enables authorities—including municipal governments (Palikas), Hello Sarkar, and the Tourist Police Nepal—to quickly identify, prioritize, and address issues based on their geographic distribution across all 77 districts of Nepal.

👥 Target Audience

Safe Nepal serves two primary user groups:

🏠

Locals (Citizen Grievance)

Residents can report potholes, illegal waste dumping, broken utility lines, and other civic issues directly on the map. These geo-tagged reports help local authorities visualize and respond to infrastructure needs efficiently.

✈️

Tourists (Travel Safety & Support)

Travelers can report unlicensed guides, blocked trekking trails, safety emergencies, or scams. Reports are routed to relevant authorities including the Tourist Police Nepal (Bhrikutimandap Headquarter).
